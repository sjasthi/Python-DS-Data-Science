## ğŸ”‘ **Key Concepts to Master**

### **Series:**
- Think of it as a **smart list** with labels
- One-dimensional (just one column of data)
- Each element has an index/label
- Perfect for storing a single type of information

### **DataFrame:**
- Think of it as a **spreadsheet in Python**
- Two-dimensional (rows AND columns)
- Like a table with labeled rows and columns
- Can store different types of data in each column
